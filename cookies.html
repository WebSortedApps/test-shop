This page explains how Test Shop uses cookies and similar technologies, in line with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make sites work, or to make them work more efficiently, and to provide information to site owners.

Cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These are required for the site to function (e.g. remembering items in your basket, keeping you logged in, recording your cookie preferences). They cannot be switched off.

  • ws_cookie_consent — stores your consent choices (first-party, 12 months)
  • Session cookies — expire when you close the browser

Analytics (optional — off by default)

If you accept, we use anonymised analytics to understand visitor behaviour (pages visited, time on site, referrer). This helps us improve the site. We do not use analytics to identify individuals.

Marketing (optional — off by default)

If you accept, we may set cookies that allow social sharing, embedded videos, or personalised advertising to work correctly.

Managing your cookies

Click the "Cookie Settings" button at the bottom of any page to change your preferences at any time. You can also clear cookies via your browser settings:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge
